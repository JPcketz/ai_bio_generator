# AI Bio Generator - Design Ideas

## Design Style Choice: **Dark Neon Gradient**

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Purple (#8B5CF6) to Turquoise (#06B6D4) gradient
- **Background**: Deep dark (#0F172A, #1E293B)
- **Accent**: Bright neon purple (#A78BFA), electric turquoise (#22D3EE)
- **Text**: White (#FFFFFF) for primary, light gray (#E2E8F0) for secondary
- **Borders**: Subtle glow effects with purple/turquoise

### Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter (Google Fonts) - modern, clean, highly legible
- **Headings**: Bold, large sizes with gradient text effects
- **Body**: Regular weight, comfortable reading size
- **CTA Buttons**: Semi-bold, uppercase for emphasis

### Visual Style
- **Layout**: Asymmetric hero section with gradient background
- **Cards**: Dark cards with subtle borders and glow effects on hover
- **Buttons**: Gradient backgrounds with hover animations
- **Shadows**: Colored shadows (purple/turquoise glow) instead of standard gray
- **Borders**: Thin, glowing borders with gradient colors
- **Animations**: Smooth transitions, subtle pulse effects on CTAs

### Component Patterns
- **Hero Section**: Full-width gradient background with centered content
- **Bio Display**: Dark card with neon border, monospace font for generated text
- **Input Fields**: Dark backgrounds with glowing focus states
- **Share Buttons**: Icon-based with gradient backgrounds
- **Ad Sections**: Clearly separated with subtle borders, non-intrusive

### Micro-interactions
- Button hover: Scale up slightly + increase glow
- Copy to clipboard: Success animation with checkmark
- Bio generation: Typing effect or fade-in animation
- Share buttons: Bounce effect on click

### Accessibility
- High contrast between text and backgrounds
- Focus states with visible outlines
- Keyboard navigation support
- ARIA labels for screen readers
